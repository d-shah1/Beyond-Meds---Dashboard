Activities

RECOMMENDED BY DOCTORS TO MAINTAIN MENTAL HEALTH.
EXERCISE

Exercise is a planned, structured and repetitive body movement done to improve or maintain physical fitness.

DEEP BREATHS

Deep breaths allow your body to fully exchange incoming oxygen with outgoing carbon dioxide. They stabilize blood pressure and lowers the stress.

TAKE A WALK

A good walk improves self-perception and self-esteem, mood and sleep quality, and it reduces stress, anxiety and fatigue.

MEDITATION

Meditation improves focus and concentration, improved self-awareness and self-esteem, lower levels of stress and anxiety, and fostering kindness.

TALK TO A FRIEND

Talking with a friend keeps us grounded and can help us get things in perspective. Talking also helps to overcome the isolation.

LIGHT A CANDLE

According to research, The warm glow of a candle has numerous benefits for someone working to improve their sleep hygiene, too.

START DRAWING OR PAINTING

According to research, Art is a great way to express your emotions without words, process complex feelings and find relief.

LISTEN TO MUSIC

Music exerts a powerful influence on human beings. It can boost memory, build task endurance, lighten your mood, reduce anxiety and depression.

COOKING NEW DISHES

Cooking is an act of patience, mindfulness, an outlet for creative expression, a means of communication, and helps to raise one’s self esteem as the cook can feel good about doing something positive for their family, themselves or loved ones.

WRITING

The act of thinking about an experience, as well as expressing emotions, seems to be important. In this way, writing helps people to organize thoughts and give meaning to a traumatic experience. Or the process of writing may enable them to regulate their emotions.
